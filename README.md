# 🎓 PocketUni: Smart Student Budgeting & Campus Guide

PocketUni is an all-in-one financial assistant and campus navigation app designed specifically for university students. It helps you manage your daily spending, find budget-friendly food spots on campus, and get AI-powered financial advice.

## 🚀 Key Features

*   **📊 Smart Budgeting:** Set your monthly income and savings goals. The "Safe Spend" engine calculates exactly how much you can spend per day.
*   **🤖 BudgetBot AI:** A smart chatbot (powered by Llama 3.3) that analyzes your spending and gives personalized financial advice.
*   **📍 Campus Recommender:** A curated list of food, stationery, and medical spots near campus with real-time directions.
*   **🛡️ Admin Panel:** A secure interface for campus leads to add or update local vendor information.
*   **📱 Mobile Ready:** Works as a website, a PWA (Install to Home Screen), or a native Android App.

## 🛠️ How to Use (For Beginners)

### 1. Set Up Your Budget
Go to the **Settings** (Gear icon). Enter your monthly allowance, your savings goal, and any fixed costs (like rent or subscriptions). The app will automatically show you your "Daily Safe Spend."

### 2. Log Expenses
Every time you spend money, click the **"+"** button. Choose the date and amount. The app will update your budget instantly.

### 3. Use the AI Chat
Got a question like "Can I afford a pizza tonight?" or "How can I save more?" Go to the **AI Chat** tab and ask BudgetBot.

### 4. Find Campus Spots
Go to the **Campus** tab to see nearby vendors. If a vendor fits within your "Daily Safe Spend," it will be highlighted with a **"✦ Fits Budget"** badge.

## 💻 Tech Stack
*   **Frontend:** React, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Lucide Icons.
*   **Database:** Supabase (Cloud Database).
*   **AI:** NVIDIA NIM (Llama 3.1/3.3).
*   **Mobile:** Capacitor (Android).
*   **Hosting:** Vercel / Netlify.
